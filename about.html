Our Heritage

Rooted in Tradition

Lisha Thai Spa was born from a deep reverence for the ancient healing arts of Thailand. Our founder's journey through the temples of Chiang Mai and the wellness centers of Bangkok inspired a vision: to bring authentic Thai therapeutic excellence to the premier spa in Bangalore.

Every technique practiced at Lisha is rooted in centuries-old tradition, refined through modern understanding of anatomy and physiology. Our therapists undergo rigorous training in traditional Nuad Thai, herbal compress therapy, and aromatherapy—ensuring each session at our massage spa in Bangalore is both culturally authentic and deeply effective.

Located on the serene 18th Cross Road in HSR Layout Sector 3, our sanctuary offers a rare escape from Bangalore's bustling energy. Conveniently situated near Koramangala, Sarjapur, and Bellandur, this premium retreat is available to you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, making us the perfect late-night spa near you.
